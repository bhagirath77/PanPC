Symbols of Brazil: The Brazilian flag is a green flag with a yellow diamond, and a blue circle in the center of the diamond. Across the circle, a green banner bears the motto Order and Progress,
The green and golden-yellow colors symbolize forests and minerals. Blue and white are Portugal's historic colors. Brazil's coat of arms commemorates the birth of the republic on Nov. 15, 1889.
