country's largest and most important defense contractors. Although in his later years Mr. Hughes retreated into a reclusive existence dominated by illness, his life was nonetheless one of
remarkable achievement. It is likely that the creation in 1953 of the medical institute that bears his name will be Mr. Hughes most enduring accomplishment. His vision for his scientific
