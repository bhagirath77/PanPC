> > >You are in: Home PVC Windows & Doors PVC Windows Parallel Balcony Parallel Balcony Special systems Hermet 10 Maximum watertightness and total security, are the advantages of
the parallel window Hermet 10. With its exclusive ironwork system, the parallel window presents/displays a very smooth and comfortable route, moving slightly the plane of the window
towards the outside. At the moment of the closing, the movable plane moves again towards the interior, being On guard closed perfectly aligned with the fixed leaf, unlike the systems of
conventional sliding windows. This system allows the window to perfectly close with the maximum watertightness, being in addition assured by several points of closing that are distributed

around all the perimeter of the frame. The parallel window of Hermet 10 is constructed with the habitual Kemmerling profiles, offering the maximum insulating benefits, easy conservation and
great durability, and in addition it offers a great variety of different finishings.
