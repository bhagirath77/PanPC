Committee for Diversity Mission Statement The primary goals of the Committee for Diversity are to increase diversity within the membership and leadership of the WSBA; to promote
opportunities for appointment or election of members to the bench; to support and encourage opportunities for minority attorneys; to aggressively pursue employment opportunities for
minorities; and to raise awareness of the benefits of diversity. Committee for Diversity Members 2008-2009 Rick Rasmussen, Co-chair Renea Saade, Co-Chair Aneelah Afzali Wilberforce
