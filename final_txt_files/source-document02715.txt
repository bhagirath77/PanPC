to collect support payments from the non-custodial parent; Any parent or person with custody of a child who needs help to establish a medical support order; Any parent or person who needs

Indiana and is not permitted to act as your attorney. Nevertheless, we may be able to assist you in the establishment, enforcement, or modification of child support orders. The Child Support
Division is not permitted to assist you in matters relating to divorce, custody, or visitation
