Story Timeline: 15 days Food Standards Agency says consumers want more information when eating out The Food Standards Agency (FSA) has announced it is to launch a pilot project
to test the feasibility of calorie labeling on menus. The trial, which will start in the summer, will see calorie information provided on menus, menu boards and other point of sale material. It will
be voluntarily adopted by a range of companies. The FSA said it wants to see more consistent nutrition information for consumers at the point they make a decision about what they eat out of
