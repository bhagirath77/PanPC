sauce, remove from the heat and let cool.Make the burgers 2. In a large bowl, combine the cooled chouti and the ground sirloin; form into 4 patties. 3. When the fire is ready, brush the grill
rack with oil. Grill the patties until desired doneness, turning once; 4 to 5 minutes per side for medium-rare; 6 to 8 minutes per side for medium. During the last few minutes of cooking, place
the rolls cut side down, on the outer edges of the grill until lightly toasted. 4. Spread the orange ali on the rolls. Cover each roll bottom with a bed of cilantro leaves, then top with a burger and
a pineapple ring. Serve immediately.
