range of top quality pediatric services for some time. As the communities we serve have grown in population and their medical needs have evolved, we are moving forward to build a state of
the art facility to house many of these services on the campus of St Alexius in Hoffman Estates. Named the Alexian Brothers Childrens Hospital this center will provide a medical home for the
area's children and offer our pediatricians and pediatric specialists a superior facility in which to practice. It will provide a focus for the pediatric services offered throughout the Network and
