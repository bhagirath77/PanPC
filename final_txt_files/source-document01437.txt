Starbucks unveils new breakfast menu options 9/3/2008 By Eric Schroeder  SEATTLE [1 Starbucks Coffee Co. today unveiled a significant change to its menu offering with the launch of five
new permanent breakfast options: Starbucks Perfect Oatmeal, Chewy Fruit & Nut Bar, Apple Bran Muffin, Berry Stella and Power Protein Plate. Starbucks said the addition of the new menu
items is the company(s next step in its commitment to providing food and beverage selections that support a healthy lifestyle. All of the items are made with wholesome ingredients such as
whole grains, real fruit, nuts and seeds, and omega-3 fatty acids. The products also do not have artificial colors, flavors, sweeteners or high-fructose corn syrup.
